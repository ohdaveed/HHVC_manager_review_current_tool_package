# Report a health nuisance or hazards (SF.gov)

- **Source title:** Report a health nuisance or hazards
- **Authority:** SF 311 / SFDPH Environmental Health
- **Original URL:** https://www.sf.gov/report-health-nuisance-or-hazards
- **Export date:** 2026-07-02
- **Phase used:** Phase 1 reporter privacy (supplementary); supports public-records framing

---

## Summary

Official 311 route for reporting environmental health issues in apartments and other spaces, including mold, overgrown weeds, and related hazards.

## Relevant policy text (extracted)

**What to know**

- You will need to give: your contact information, type of property and address, details about your concern.
- Response time varies.
- **Complaints are public records. Your name is usually not shared for complaints about environmental health.**

## Audit relevance

Supports reporter-privacy copy that addresses public records. Combined with program manager attestation, mockup copy may state that reporter identities are only shared with the City Attorney's Office and are not shared in response to public records requests.
