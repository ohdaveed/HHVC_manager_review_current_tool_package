## Late payment penalty

- $10 after 30 days
- $30 after 60 days

## Healthy Housing reinspection fees (FY 2024–25)

- Reinspection by environmental health inspector, per hour: $244
- Reinspection by environmental health technician, per hour: $223
- Additional fee for reinspections requiring more than one hour — each half hour: $122 (inspector) / $110 (technician)
