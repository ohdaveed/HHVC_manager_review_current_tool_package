What changes structurally

  • Topic page's direct fan-out drops from 16 to 11 — the 6 report links move one level deeper, behind a page whose whole job is "here's how reporting works, pick your pest"
  • Still real navigation, not just a UI trick: a person landing on the topic page sees fewer choices up front, then a focused set of report options once they signal intent to report
  • This is the same "Report or pay" grouping the tool already infers visually (getCluster()) — the hub would make that grouping real in the data, not just a display heuristic
