| --- | --- |

| 16–20 | $475 |
| 21–30 | $673 |
| Over 30 | $791 |

## Healthy Housing reinspection fees

- Reinspection by environmental health inspector, per hour: $251
- Reinspection by environmental health technician, per hour: $229
- Additional fee for reinspections requiring more than one hour — each additional half hour: $125 (inspector) / $113 (technician)

## Late payment penalty

- $10 after 30 days
- $30 after 60 days
- Interest accrues at 1.5% per month from date of recordation (Refuse Lien Program rate; applied citywide by EHB to unpaid balances)
