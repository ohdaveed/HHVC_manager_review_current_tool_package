## Safe to adopt (cross-confirmed)

- Tenant **written notice** to property owner or manager before 311
- **72-hour wait** when possible before 311 (program-manager rule)
- Landlord **72-hour investigation** (Director's Rules §V.2 legacy PDF)
- **12-inch** furniture pull-back from walls for unit prep (prep guidance, not statute)

## Do not adopt without legal review

- Repair & deduct limits
- Six-month retaliation window wording
- Full escalation ladder as enforcement procedure
