## Three distinct timelines (do not conflate)

1. **Tenant 72-hour written notice** before 311 — program-manager rule + Compliance xlsx (not Article 11A alone).
2. **Owner 72-hour investigation** — Director's Rules §V.2 (legacy PDF until v4.0 §V restored).
3. **Bed bug PCO response** — Article 11A / bed bug Director's Rules: **two working days** (not 72 hours).

## Adjacent-unit inspection

Legacy §VI.6: *"All units adjacent to the infested unit shall be inspected and treated if necessary."*

This is a **general PCO duty**, not bed-bug-only. Bed bugs add the separate two-working-day timeline and K9/adjoining-room operational detail (Pestec).
