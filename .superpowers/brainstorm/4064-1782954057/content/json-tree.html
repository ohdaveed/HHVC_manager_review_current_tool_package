Pros

  • Directly attacks "too much text/data" — collapsed clusters show almost nothing, you open only what you're inspecting
  • One page's links (in AND out) unfold inline right where you're looking, no separate detail panel needed
  • Familiar interaction pattern (file tree / JSON viewer) — no new mental model to teach reviewers
  • Scales well: 40 pages collapsed is still a short list; only expanded branches take space

Cons

  • Still text-based at the leaf level (→2 ←1) unless combined with dot icons from Option A
  • Loses the at-a-glance "shape of the whole site" that a diagram (Option B) gives you
  • Biggest rebuild of the three visual directions — replaces the current two-column tree+detail layout, not just the badges
