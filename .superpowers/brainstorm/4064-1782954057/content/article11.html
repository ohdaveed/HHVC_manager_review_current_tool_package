Understanding Health Code Article 11

Article 11 is the San Francisco law that requires property owners to keep buildings free of pests and health hazards. This page explains it in plain language. Reading target: grade 6–8.

What property owners must do

Keep the building free of things that can harm health — mold, garbage, rodents, and insects. Also handle hazards like lead and asbestos.

What tenants must do

Keep your unit reasonably clean and orderly. This helps prevent pests from moving in.

What counts as "vermin"

Rodents, bed bugs, cockroaches, pigeons, and mosquitoes — anything that can spread disease, damage property, or contaminate food.

How this gets paid for
